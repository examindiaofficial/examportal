Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information

📅 Effective Date: March 1, 2025  ·  Last Updated: March 1, 2025

👋 Introduction

Welcome to ExamIndia ("we", "our", or "us"). We operate the website examindia.in (the "Service"), a free platform that helps students prepare for Indian government examinations.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data. By using ExamIndia, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

📋 Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Name and email address when you create an account
  • WhatsApp number if you subscribe to exam alerts
  • Profile details such as age, education, category, and state

Information collected automatically:

  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Cookies and local storage data (for saved exams and preferences)

🎯 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Send you exam deadline alerts and notifications you subscribed to
  • Personalise your experience (e.g. eligibility checker, saved exams)
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Analyse usage patterns to improve the platform
  • Display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense

📢 Advertising — Google AdSense

ExamIndia uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings or by visiting aboutads.info.

For more information about how Google uses data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

🔥 Firebase (Google) Services

We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore (by Google) to manage user accounts and store your preferences securely. Firebase may collect certain device and usage data as described in Firebase's Privacy Policy.

🤖 AI Features

ExamIndia uses the Anthropic Claude API to power the AI chat assistant and mock test generator. When you use these features, your questions are sent to Anthropic's servers to generate responses. We do not store your AI conversation history on our servers. Anthropic processes data as described in their Privacy Policy.

🍪 Cookies & Local Storage

We use cookies and browser local storage for:

  • Keeping you signed in to your account
  • Saving your exam preferences and notification settings
  • Remembering your language preference
  • Google AdSense advertising cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of the site.

🔒 Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information. User accounts are secured through Firebase Authentication using industry-standard encryption. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

👦 Children's Privacy

ExamIndia is intended for students who are at least 16 years of age (minimum eligibility age for most government exams). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

🔗 Third-Party Links

Our platform contains links to official government websites (e.g. ssc.gov.in, upsc.gov.in) and third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before submitting any personal information.

✏️ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Unsubscribe from exam notifications at any time
  • Opt out of personalised advertising

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
